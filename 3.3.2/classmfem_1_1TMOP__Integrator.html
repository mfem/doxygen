Detailed Description

A TMOP integrator class based on any given TMOP_QualityMetric and TargetConstructor.

Represents over a target zone, where W is the metric's strain energy density function, and Jpt is the Jacobian of the target->physical coordinates transformation. The virtual target zone is defined by the TargetConstructor.

Detailed Description

Class for integrating the bilinear form a(u,v) := - (Q u, grad v) in either 2D or 3D and where Q is an optional coefficient (of type scalar, matrix, or diagonal matrix) u is in H(Div) or H(Curl) and v is in H1.
